How to Enter

1. Fill the form with your actual information.

2. If you fill the form right, you'll recieve an acceptance email from our team.

3. Join our Discord Server using the link inside your acceptance email.

4. After you joined our Discord Server, head over to the #readme channel for further information.

Event Rules

1. The participants should be a student in Ummul Quro Highschool.

2. Participants need to be focus while following the series of events in DIGIFEST.

3. Participants should be ready to spare their time for DIGIFEST.

4. When inside a Zoom/Google Meet/Discord meeting, participants need to open their webcam. If there are any problems with the connection or etc, please report to the mentor.

5. Participants who forgot about the rules are going to be reminded.

6. For those who are deliberately dismissed the rules are going to be kicked out from the event.

7. Always follow the main school rules while entering this event.
